Weather Database

This team project was to design and create a database using MySQL. The dataset we chose was from Kaggle and consisted of weather data from major cities in the world. As weather is a globally relevant phenomenon and climate change being a hot topic, we thought that a weather database would be useful to both scientists and the general public. This weather data included humidity, pressure, temperature, weather description, wind direction, and wind speed. In addition, we created some of our own data such as altitude, precipitation, air quality, and cloudiness. Due to the enormous amount of data within the Kaggle dataset, we reduced the number of cities to just 10 for manageability. Furthermore, we decided to limit our date/time records to 500 hours worth of data. The whole project took careful planning, drafting, normalizing, and importing.
